Privacy Policy for ScoreCounter

Effective Date: January 5, 2026

1. Introduction

ScoreCounter ("we," "our," or "us") respects the privacy of our users ("user" or "you"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application, ScoreCounter (the "Application").

2. Collection of Data

Personal Data

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number.

Device and Usage Data (Automatically Collected)

The Application may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to:

  • Device type (e.g., Samsung Galaxy, Pixel)
  • Operating system version (e.g., Android 13)
  • Unique device identifiers, including the Advertising ID
  • Approximate Location (inferred from your IP address)
  • App Performance Data (e.g., crash logs, diagnostics)
  • App Activity (e.g., app interactions, ad views)

Advertising ID

We use the Google Advertising ID (AAID) to display personalized advertisements and measure ad performance.

3. Use of Data

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To Provide Services: To maintain the core functionality of the app (e.g., saving game scores locally on your device).
  • Advertising: To display third-party advertisements via Google AdMob. This helps support the development and maintenance of the Application.
  • Analytics: To measure the performance of advertisements and the application itself (e.g., view counts, click-through rates, crash reporting).

4. Third-Party Services

We may share information with the following third-party service provider:

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display ads. Google AdMob may collect and use your Advertising ID, approximate location, and other device information to serve personalized ads.

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit Google's Privacy & Terms .

5. Data Retention

  • Game Data: All game scores and team names are stored locally on your device using an SQLite database. We do not transmit or store this data on external servers.
  • Ad Data: Data collected by third-party advertisers (like Google) is retained according to their own privacy policies.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Application is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we will be able to do necessary actions.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
